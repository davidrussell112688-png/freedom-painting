WHAT WE DO

OUR SERVICES

★

From full exterior repaints to detailed cabinet refinishing, we handle every brush stroke with care. No shortcuts. No cut corners.

🏠

EXTERIOR PAINTING

Siding, trim, shutters, and full exterior repaints. We prep thoroughly — scraping, sanding, priming — because paint only looks as good as the surface underneath. We work on everything from historic Victorians to modern builds.

🎨

INTERIOR PAINTING

Walls, ceilings, trim, doors, and accent walls. We protect your furniture, cover your floors, and leave zero mess behind. Crisp lines, smooth finishes, and colors that transform your space.

🍳

CABINET REFINISHING

Give your kitchen a facelift without a full renovation. We sand, prime, and paint cabinets with durable finishes that hold up to daily use. A fraction of the cost of replacement.

🪵

DECK & FENCE STAINING

Restore your deck or fence to like-new condition. We power wash, prep, and apply high-quality stains and sealers that protect against Maine's harsh weather year after year.

🔨

WALL REPAIR & PREP

Cracks, holes, water damage, imperfections — we fix it all before any paint goes on. Proper prep is what separates a paint job that lasts from one that peels in a year.

🏡

COLOR CONSULTATION

Not sure what color to go with? We help you choose palettes that complement your home's architecture, your neighborhood, and your personal taste. We'll get it right.
